Synesthesia

This project explores the translation of art across sensory mediums, reinterpreting the source material to create new layers of meaning. By transforming famous artworks into alternate forms—sound, touch, movement—it creates an accessible means of interpretation. Synesthesia's digital archive seeks to dissolve limitations, allowing diverse audiences to experience art beyond its original form.
